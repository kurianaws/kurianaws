Electricuit

"Bug the bugs"

We will break it up for you. You have to put it back. Race against the clock and fellow nerds to do it first and do it right.

In association with TRIKA 2K20, Electronics and Communication Department of ER and DCI Institute of Technology, Academic wing of CDAC, presents

“ELECTRICUIT” Circuit Debugging Contest

Rules

  • A team of at most two shall participate.
  • Graduate and Postgraduate Degree students can participate.

Competition

  • 1st round - 30 multiple choice questions related to electronic circuits. 60 min time duration. 4 marks each question. 1 mark negative marking.
  • 6 teams to be selected from prelims. Malfunctioning circuit will be given in LTSpice. Each team will have to fix the circuit and show the output. First two teams who do it will get the prize in the order that they finish.
Participating teams need to know LTSpice.
