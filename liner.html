Rules and Regulations

  • No purchased robots are allowed, only robot made by the team is permitted.
  • The robots must be autonomous and must not be controlled using wired or wireless methods.
  • The track will have multiple checkpoints and clearing each earns points.
  • The result will be based on timing and points earned by the robot from different checkpoints.
  • There will be two rounds and best of two will be considered.
  • The result once declared is final.
  • Participate in team of 1-4.
  • ID card of respective institute is mandatory for all participants.
