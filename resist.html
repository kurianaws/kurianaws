As a part of Trika 2020, we present to you

"Resist the Flow" If you believe that you know the basic Ohms law and you are good with numbers, then this is for you!!!

Rules

  • This is an individual event consisting of 2 rounds.
  • First and second prizes will be awarded.

Competiton:

  • Round 1: It will be a written test whereby the participants are required to obtain a voltage value from a given source voltage and resistors. The first 5 to obtain the value will be chosen for the next round.
  • Round 2: Resistors will be provided to the chosen participants and they are to obtain the given resistance value. The fastest ones win.
